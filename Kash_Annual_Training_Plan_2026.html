Bowling Drills (Wed/Fri Sessions)

From Cricket SA drill library + biomechanics research. Rent nets for these sessions.

1

Line & Length

Target: 1m × 30cm area on good length. 6 overs of 6 balls. Start half-pace, build to full pace. Track accuracy %.

2

Seam Control (Aggot Drill)

Hold ball with seam upright, bowl at single stump. Focus on landing seam on the pitch. 4 overs. Count seam-up deliveries.

3

Red/White Ball Drill

Alternate red and white balls each delivery. Forces focus on grip adjustment between balls. 6 overs total.

4

Using the Crease

Bowl from wide of crease, then tight to stumps. Vary angles to create different lines. 4 overs each position.

5

Yorker Practice

Mark 50cm strip at batting crease. 6 overs targeting the crease line. Track hit percentage.

6

Bouncer Practice

Cross-seam, targeting above waist. Focus on back-of-a-length consistency. 4 overs.

7

Fatigued Bowling

10× run-ups (no ball) then immediately bowl 1 over. Repeat 4–6 times. Tests technique under match fatigue.

8

Match Simulation

2 batsmen in, 4-over spell. Bowl as if in a match — set fields mentally, execute plans under pressure.

Korean Food Rulebook Eat this, avoid that
✅ EXCELLENT — Daily OK
Samgyetang (ginseng chicken soup) — 600 cal, 55g P · whole chicken in broth, clean
Bulgogi (marinated beef grilled) — 450 cal, 40g P · ask less sauce, skip rice
Galbi (grilled ribs) — 500 cal, 45g P · lean cuts, grill yourself
Dak Galbi (spicy stir-fried chicken) — 450 cal, 50g P · no noodles
Samgyeopsal (grilled pork belly) — 600 cal, 35g P · fatty but protein-heavy, limit portion
Sundubu Jjigae (soft tofu stew) — 350 cal, 25g P · tofu + egg + seafood
Mul Naengmyeon (cold buckwheat noodles) — 450 cal, 15g P · lighter than most noodles
Gyeranjjim (steamed egg casserole) — 150 cal, 12g P · pure egg protein side
Kimchi + banchan — <50 cal total · near-zero cal, gut health
⚠️ MODERATE — 2–3× during trip, portion-control
Bibimbap — rice-heavy but balanced · eat half the rice, full protein + veg
Korean Fried Chicken — delicious but calorie bomb · share a plate, 4–5 pcs max, no beer
Jajangmyeon (black bean noodles) — heavy · share, eat with extra kimchi
Tteokbokki (spicy rice cakes) — all carbs · small portion, share, add fishcake for protein
Korean BBQ buffet — can go either way · focus on meat, minimal rice, lettuce wraps
🚫 AVOID OR LIMIT HEAVILY
Soju / Makgeolli / Beer — Korean drinking culture is intense. Decline respectfully. If forced: soju (124 cal/shot) ≥ beer > makgeolli
Bingsu (shaved ice dessert) — 800–1,200 cal. Skip.
Tonkatsu (deep-fried pork cutlet) — ~700 cal per piece
Ramyeon / instant noodles — high sodium, empty cal
Street-food fried items — hotteok, twigim
Convenience store ddeok / breads — carb-heavy, low protein

Hotel gym hacks
  • If DBs max out at 15 kg — do higher reps (12–15) or slower tempo (3-sec eccentric).
